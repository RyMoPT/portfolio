About


I always liked to build things and help others.

I wanted to be a healthcare practitioner because I wanted to help others and make a difference in people's lives.

So I dedicated my post-secondary education to pursuing a career in physiotherapy.

After becoming a physiotherapist, I realized I still had that need to build and develop things.

One day I thought, why not do both?

Through resources such as The Odin Project, MDN, and YouTube, I started learning everything I needed to develop the things I wanted to create.

Being self taught has allowed me to learn effectively, efficiently, and quickly. I am constantly learning new concepts, tools, and languages in order to develop whatever ideas come to mind.
